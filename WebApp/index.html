Welcome to our Cancer Forums and Support Groups

According to WHO stats there are 17 million new cases of cancer worldwide in 2018. After being diagnosed with cancer, people find it difficult to cope up with the news and usually go through emotional challenges and mental anxiety.

CancerBro is an online platform for people affected by cancer to connect with each other. Having cancer is a stressful experience in a person’s life. But our cancer forums and support groups help people impacted by disease to cope up emotionally by sharing allowing them to share their experiences and feelings with others who can totally understand what they are going through.

Sometimes it is difficult or weird for people living with cancer to share their feelings and experiences with their family, friends, and loved ones. Thus, in our online support communities anyone can share anything either publicly or privately via direct messages.

We have 24 support groups and forums for major cancer types including: breast cancer, lung cancer, brain cancer, lymphoma, testicular cancer, etc. You can find the list of our cancer forums and support groups here.

Our global community is growing on a daily basis and people are joining their hands with us for raising cancer awareness and empowering others are impacted by cancer. Most of our community members are from the United States, India, UK, Canada, and other major countries in the world.

Our Cancer Social Media Platform makes it easier for anyone with cancer or looking for support to connect with cancer survivors. Thus, making it one of the best cancer survivors network online.
